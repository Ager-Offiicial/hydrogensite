Frequently Asked Questions

            

            

                

                    
What operating systems does Hydrogen support?

                    
Hydrogen is designed to run in any modern terminal environment on macOS, Linux, and Windows (via WSL or native terminals that support ANSI escape codes).

                

                

                    
What Ollama models are recommended?

                    
Hydrogen works well with a variety of models. For a good balance of speed and capability, models like Phi-3 Medium, Llama 3 8B Instruct, or Mistral 7B Instruct are excellent choices. Larger models can provide more nuanced responses but require more resources.

                

                

                    
Is my code sent to any external servers?

                    
No. When using Hydrogen with Ollama, all AI processing happens locally on your machine. Your code and data remain private.

                

                

                    
What if I buy the Ignition License and want updates later?

                    
The Ignition License grants you access to the version of Hydrogen available at the time of purchase. To receive ongoing updates and new features, you would need to purchase a new Ignition License for a future major version or subscribe to the Fusion plan.
